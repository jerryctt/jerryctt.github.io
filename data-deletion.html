User Data Deletion Instructions

Last updated:

TripScribe uses third-party sign-in (Google / Apple / Facebook) only to create a session for you. We do not collect user-generated content. At most, we store:

  • Authentication identifiers from your identity provider (e.g., sub/id).
  • Email address, only if the provider supplies it and only for account/login purposes.
  • Short-lived session information (cookies/tokens).

How to stop processing / become anonymous

  1. Sign out from the service. This clears your session so the service runs in an anonymous state.
  2. (Optional) Revoke our app’s access at your identity provider:
    • Facebook: Settings → Apps and Websites → Remove access to “TripScribe”.
    • Google: myaccount.google.com → Security → Third-party access.
    • Apple: appleid.apple.com → Sign-In & Security → Sign in with Apple.

Request deletion of stored identifiers (if any)

If you want us to remove the minimal identifiers we keep (e.g., provider user ID and/or email, if present), please email us at Jerry Chan with the subject “Request to Delete Account and Personal Data”. Include the sign-in method you used (Google / Apple / Facebook).

After verifying your request, we will delete or anonymize your data within a reasonable period (typically within 30 days), except where retention is required by law or for security, audit
