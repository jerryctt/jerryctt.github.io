This Privacy Policy explains how TripScribe (“we”, “us”, or “our”) handles personal information. We use third-party sign-in (Google / Apple / Facebook) solely to create and maintain your session. We do not collect user-generated content or behavioral analytics.

Information We Collect

  • Authentication identifiers from your identity provider (e.g., sub/id).
  • Email address, only if provided by the identity provider and only for account/login purposes.
  • Short-lived session data (cookies/tokens) required to keep you signed in.

How We Use Information

  • Authenticate users and maintain secure sessions.
  • Protect our service (fraud prevention, troubleshooting, and security).
  • Communicate essential service or security notices when needed.

Cookies

We use only essential cookies required for authentication and security. Disabling them may prevent you from signing in.

Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only with service providers that host or operate our service, under appropriate safeguards, or when required by law or to protect rights and safety.

Data Retention

Session cookies are short-lived. Minimal account identifiers (provider user ID and, if present, email) are retained only as needed to operate sign-in. You may request deletion at any time as described on our User Data Deletion page.

Your Choices and Rights

  • Revoke our access at your identity provider (Google / Apple / Facebook).
  • Request access to or deletion of stored identifiers by emailing us at jerrycttdev@gmail.com.

Children’s Privacy

Our service is not directed to children. If you believe we collected data from a minor, contact us and we will act promptly.

International Transfers

Your information may be processed in countries other than your own. We apply reasonable safeguards to protect it.
